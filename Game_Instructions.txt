** Click character portrait to select **

** Score = Coins collected for now. **

** Green Bar = Health **

** Blue Bar = Magic (can't use magic when it reached 0) **

** GEM POWERS**
You can only choose two!

*Earth (orange gem)
-Flower of life - Restores health.

*Wind (green gem)
-Fly - Self explanory ;-)

*Fire (red gem)
-Fire Boost - Boosts speed, jump, and max health and magic.

*Water (blue gem)
-Piercing Water Crystal - Pierces through walls to attack enemies!


** Controls **

-KeyBoard controls
------------------
<- = Left
-> = Right
^ =  Jump / Enter Door

'A' attacks with a punch
'S' shoots magic
'Z' Flower of life
'W' Fly
'X' Piercing Water Crystal

-XBOX 360 controller controls
----------------------------
*Left Analog
<- = Left
-> = Right
'A' = Jump / Enter Door

'X' attacks with a punch
'B' shoots magic
